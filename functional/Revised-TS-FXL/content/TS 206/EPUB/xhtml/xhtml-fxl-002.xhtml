[REQUIRED] fxl-060 Fixed layout pages in landscape mode

Tests whether setting the global rendition:orientation property to landscape results in pages always being rendered in landscape mode.

If the pages of this publication are rendered in landscape mode, regardless of how the device is held, the test passes. If the pages are rendered in portrait mode, the test fails.

In other words, the direction of the downward reading direction is parallel to the short axis of the device, no matter what the orientation.
