[OPTIONAL] fxl-070 Fixed layouts with pages in portrait mode

Tests whether setting the global rendition:orientation property to portrait results in pages always being rendered in portrait mode.

If the pages of this publication are rendered in portrait mode, regardless of how the device is held, the test passes. If the pages are rendered in landscape mode, the test fails.

In other words, the direction of the downward reading direction is parallel to the long axis of the device, no matter what the orientation, the test passes.
